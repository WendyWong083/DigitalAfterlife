Creative Expressions:
Art, Film, and AI Memorials

Explore how AI reshapes memory and mourning in speculative art and sci-fi. Each creative work below invites us to imagine the emotional weight of digital resurrection and the limits of identity.

Eternal You (2024)

Startups use AI to create avatars that allow people to talk with their loved ones after death. Exploration of deep human desire for immortality, questioning consequences of commodifying this age-old dream.

Marjorie Prime (2017)

In a future where people purchase interactive holograms of deceased loved ones, this film examines how memory and identity are reshaped by artificial substitutes.
