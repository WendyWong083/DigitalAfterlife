Emotional Implications:

Healing or Holding On Too Tightly?

Interview with psychotherapist Alli Ruttger

In this interview, we spoke to Alli Ruttger, a trauma-trained psychotherapist and EMDR therapist based in Hong Kong, to explore the psychological implications of these AI technologies in reshaping grief.

Together they discussed what 'virtual reunions' mean for the grieving process and how to strike a healthy balance between remembering and moving on.

Whether you're curious about the future of digital memory or navigating your own journey with grief, this conversation offers insight, comfort and a deeper understanding of what it means to love and lose in the digital age.
